About me

Hello! I'm Abhimanyu Gupta. People call me Abhi.

I currenly work at Microsoft on the Core OS Data and Intelligence team in Redmond, WA. We provide data and analytics for the Windows Networking team, specifically as they relate to mobile connectivity (Wi-Fi, Cellular, and IoT devices).

I graduated from Cornell University studying Computer Science and Business. Here, I had the pleasure of working with great people on projects relating to music recommendation, virtual voice assistants, and solar panel placement in cities. Many of my projects involve machine learning (both supervised and unsupervised). This includes Neural Network implementations (DeepNets & RNN's), Reinforcement Learning, and Probabalistic Modeling (Hidden Markov & Graphical Models).

If you love sports (especially football), music, coffee, or all of the above, we will definitely get along. I am a huge New England Patriots fan, and I love all teams Boston. It's also impossible for me to get through a day without listening to music. Check out what I'm listening to!
