About Words

Words: Articles and thoughts curated by Abhi Gupta

Words is a place for me to explore my thoughts, including topics I find interesting and believe others should be aware of. Too often technical content is very difficult to understand. Filled wth math and terms a reader may not have heard of, it's easy to see why people may be hesitant to dive into a topic. I aim to provide easy to read, less-technical (or entirely non-technical) explanations of concepts that anyone can pick up.
